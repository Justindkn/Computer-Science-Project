What is Vindinium?

Vindinium is an AI contest in which you create an AI that controls one of four "heroes" on the map. Each AI, or "bot" has 100 health. Scattered around the map are "mines" and "taverns." Standing next to a mine will claim it; however, you will lose 20 health. Standing next to a tavern will restore 50 health, at the cost of 2 gold. Each turn the bot will move one tile, lose one health due to "thirst", and get one "gold" from each mine they own. The objective of the game is to have the most gold by the end of a preset number of turns.

So what does my Vindinium Bot do?

Basically, my bot does the following things:

  • Run to a tavern if it's below 60 hp
  • Attack a bot if it's within 5 tiles of my bot and has less health than my bot.
  • Run away to a tavern if an enemy bot is within 4 tiles of my bot and has more health than my bot
  • Attack a botmine if it has more than 60 hp
  • Attack a freemine if no enemy bots / mines are availible.

How does it do it?

Overview

  1. In the first section of my code, I defined a few variables and my arrays.

  2. Next, I created an if , else if , else statement. I gave each statement a task to complete under certain conditions.

  3. Finally, in the last section (3) I assigned a function to each task. If the task was chosen then it would perform that funcion.
